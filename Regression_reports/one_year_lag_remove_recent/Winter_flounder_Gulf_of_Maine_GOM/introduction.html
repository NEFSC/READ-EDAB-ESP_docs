1 Introduction

These are preliminary regressions that compare Gulf of Maine Winter flounder catch, abundance, recruitment, and F to various indicators in the GOM, All, all, NE Environmental Protection Units (EPUs) taken from the ecodata package. The indicators are lagged by 1 years.
